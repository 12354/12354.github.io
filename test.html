Thief

The Thief is a recurring enemy in Shattered Pixel Dungeon, appearing in the Prison. They are relatively weak, but can be dangerous due to their ability to steal items from the player.

Appearance

The Thief is depicted as a skeletal figure with a hooded cloak and a dagger. They are often seen carrying a stolen item.

Abilities

  • Steal: The Thief has a chance to steal an item from the player’s inventory. This chance is higher if the Thief is carrying an item. The Thief will only steal items that are not unique and have a level of +0.
  • Poison: The Thief has a chance to inflict poison on the player when they attack. The amount of poison inflicted scales with the Thief’s level.

AI

The Thief’s AI is relatively simple, but it can be tricky to predict their actions. Here is a breakdown of their AI states and how they transition between them:

States:

  • SLEEPING: The Thief starts in a sleeping state. They will not act until they are awakened.
  • WANDERING: The Thief will wander randomly around the level. They will not attack unless they see the player.
  • HUNTING: The Thief will actively pursue the player. They will attack if they are in range.
  • FLEEING: The Thief will run away from the player. They will only attack if they are cornered.

State Transitions:

Conditions:

  • See Player: The Thief will awaken if they see the player.
  • In Range: The Thief will attack if they are adjacent to the player.
  • Steal Item: The Thief will attempt to steal an item from the player if they are carrying an item.
  • Cornered: The Thief will attack if they are surrounded by walls or other obstacles.
  • No Terror/Dread: The Thief will stop fleeing and resume hunting if they are not affected by Terror or Dread.

Strategies

  • Keep your distance: The Thief is more likely to steal items if they are close to you. Try to keep a safe distance and avoid getting cornered.
  • Use Invisibility: The Thief cannot steal items from an invisible player.
  • Kill them quickly: The Thief is relatively weak, so try to kill them before they can steal anything.
  • Be aware of their poison: The Thief’s poison can be dangerous, so be sure to heal yourself if you are poisoned.

Notes

  • The Thief is a relatively common enemy, so be prepared to encounter them frequently.
  • The Thief’s AI is not perfect, and they can sometimes make mistakes.
  • The Thief is a good source of Rings and Artifacts, so it is worth killing them if you can.

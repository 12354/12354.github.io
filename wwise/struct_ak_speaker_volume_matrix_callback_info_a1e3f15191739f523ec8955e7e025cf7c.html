AkReal32* AkSpeakerVolumeMatrixCallbackInfo::pfEmitterListenerVolume

Emitter-listener pair-specific gain. When there are multiple emitter-listener pairs, this volume is set to that of the loudest pair, and the relative gain of other pairs is applied directly on the channel volume matrix pVolumes.
